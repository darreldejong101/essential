About Us

Background

Essential Gaming is a gaming company which was founded by the Owner Darryl De Jong and the Co-Owner Abhishek Dey. It was founded this year in the Information Technology Advanced class at the Hamliton Boys' High School's computer lab. We make and produce games at the fastest rate that we possibly can and relie on the feedback of stakeholders to produce the best game physically possible. We Look forward to reaching a massive milestone with over one million players registered to our player base.

What We Do

Here at Essential Gaming we make produce games to please our stakeholders and entertain them the best can. Our games are free but customisations can be bought from the their ingame stores. Customisations include, Skin Customisations, Theme Customisations, Target Customisations, Perks/Bonuses. We will add special events on important days of the year for example an event for Christmas.
